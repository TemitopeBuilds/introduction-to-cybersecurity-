# introduction-to-cybersecurity-
    ## What is cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, devices, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, theft, or damage.

    ## Why is cybersecurity important 
Cybersecurity helps protect:

* Personal information (passwords, bank accounts, photos)
* Businesses from hackers and data breaches
* Government systems and critical infrastructure
* Online services such as email, shopping, and banking

        ## Examples
-Banks
-Schools
-Government agencies
-Business
